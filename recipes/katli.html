Kaju Katli

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cup cashew / kaju
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. take 2 cup cashew and blend to a fine powder. make sure to pulse and blend else, the cashew will release oil and turns into a paste.
  2. sieve the cashew powder making sure there are no lumps. keep aside
  3. in a large kadai take 1 cup sugar and ½ cup water.
  4. stir well and dissolve sugar. boil for 5 minutes or until string consistency is attained, add in powdered cashew and mix well.
  5. continue to cook until the mixture turns smooth paste and starts to separate pan slightly. do not overcook, as the burfi will turn hard.transfer the mixture on to the butter paper. make sure to grease the butter paper with ghee.
  6. now fold using a spatula until the mixture thickens forming a dough.once the dough is formed, knead slightly, to form a soft dough.
  7. place the cashew dough between butter paper and roll using a rolling pin.roll slightly thick making sure it's uniform.cut into diamond shape or shape of your choice.
