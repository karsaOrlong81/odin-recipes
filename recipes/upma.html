Tomato Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sooji / Rava (Semolina)
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 tomatos chopped fine
  • Spices for tempering
  • 1 Green chilli slit
  • 1tsp Ginger and garlic paste

Instructions

  1. Roast the rava on medium to low heat and set aside
  2. Add mustard, jeera, chana dal, urad dal, chilli, and saut√©
  3. Add hing, red chilli, green chilli, ginger, garlic and saute
  4. Add onions, carrot and curry leaves, Add tomato
  5. Stir, cover and cook till tomatoes soften
  6. Add salt and water
  7. Add the rava, stir until water is absorbed
